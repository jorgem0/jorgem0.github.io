I have documented information on how to do various projects so I can look back at this website as a reference. I have also added the "Other" links to my website which contain my interests that do not require tutorials. These links serve as a place that I can put pictures that I have taken so that they aren't just on my phone. Please feel free to look around and use the tutorials.
